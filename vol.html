Programs At Jeevan Ankur Trust

Our NGO offers a variety of programs aimed at supporting and empowering the community. From education and healthcare to environmental conservation and skill development, we address diverse needs to make a positive impact.

Below, you will find detailed information about each of our specific programs, highlighting our ongoing efforts and achievements in various areas.

Programs

Children Education

Our Children Education program focuses on providing quality education to underprivileged children. We offer free tuition, school supplies, and extracurricular activities to ensure a holistic learning experience. Our goal is to nurture young minds and pave the way for a brighter future.

Women Empowerment

The Women Empowerment program is dedicated to uplifting women by providing them with vocational training, financial literacy workshops, and leadership development opportunities. We aim to foster independence, boost self-confidence, and create pathways for economic self-sufficiency.

Sahara Program

The Sahara Program for Senior Citizens offers comprehensive support to the elderly, including healthcare services, recreational activities, and social engagement opportunities. Our mission is to enhance their quality of life, promote mental and physical well-being, and ensure they feel valued and respected.

Food Program

Our Food Program addresses food insecurity by providing nutritious meals to those in need. We operate community kitchens, distribute food packages, and run awareness campaigns on healthy eating. Our efforts ensure that everyone has access to essential nourishment and lives a healthier life.

Medical Help To The Needy

Our Medical Help to the Needy program provides essential healthcare services to underserved communities. We offer free medical check-ups, medications, and health education to those who lack access to proper healthcare. Our aim is to improve health outcomes and ensure everyone receives the care they need to lead healthy lives.

Internships

Our internship program offers hands-on experience in various fields within our NGO. Interns have the opportunity to work on real projects, gain valuable skills, and contribute to meaningful initiatives. We provide mentorship, training, and a supportive environment to help interns grow both personally and professionally
