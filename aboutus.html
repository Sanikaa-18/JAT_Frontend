ABOUT US

Mission

To uplift and support vulnerable individuals in society, especially the elderly, needy, and women, by providing essential resources, compassionate care, and empowerment opportunities. Through dedicated service, we aim to restore dignity, foster independence, and bring hope to those who need it most.

Values

• Empathy & Respect – Valuing individual dignity
• Integrity & Honesty – Transparency in all actions
• Community Bonding – Strengthening social ties
• Empowerment & Growth – Enabling self-sufficiency
• Compassionate Service – Serving with kindness

Vision

A compassionate and inclusive society where every individual, regardless of age, gender, or circumstance, can live a life of dignity, independence, and purpose. Jeevan Ankur Trust envisions a future where communities are empowered, women are self-reliant, and the elderly and needy are valued and supported.

Our approach

Our Story

We reach out to children and make them interact with young people from universities and teach them in a friendly way so as to provide them with a family environment for learning, make them understand our culture and hardships they need to overcome in the journey of their life.Our main motives are making children aware of the current scenario in which the world exists and train them toovercome the taboos of society. We also make them aware of the social issues they are in and how to make it a betterplace for them to survive and grow.A well defined smile is what we care for and try to achieve with our constant approach.
