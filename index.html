The Rising People Welfare Society

WHO WE ARE AND WHAT DO WE DO?

The rising people welfare society aims to build a better living ecosystem to the underprivileged childeren in New Delhi by providing them with the right education and care at the right time in life.

WE TEACH THEM THE RIGHT WAY

TRPWS is a joint programme with the mission to empower and support underprivileged chidren by ensuring that every child has at least 1 adult assigned to them.

Donation drives

One man's trash is another man's treasure. Our needy children get donations everymonth from the privileged section. You never know, a pen to you, is worth a career to another.

Teaching

In todays world, everyone needs to be educated. To survive in this world, More literacy isn't enough. Our kids are taught alphabets to alpha beta gama. We make sure that at the end of the day, the child doesn't only have fun but learn somthing of value.

Ending drugmenace

We belive that drugs and addiction are a pediment in a child's future. Our attempts to divert children from the wrong path and get rid of any addiction have been successful so far.
